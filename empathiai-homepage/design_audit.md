# EmpathAI Homepage Design Audit

## 1. Overall Design Theme
- **Style:** Modern, Clean, Educational, Approachable
- **Visual Elements:** 
  - Soft gradients (Purple/Lavender dominant)
  - Glassmorphism effects (Backdrop blur, translucent backgrounds)
  - Rounded corners (Buttons, Cards, Sections)
  - Card-based layouts with shadows for depth
- **Typography:** 'Lora' (Serif) for a trustworthy and academic feel.
